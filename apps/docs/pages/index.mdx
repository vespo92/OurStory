# OurStory Research Agents

Welcome to the OurStory Research Agents documentation. This platform hosts specialized AI research agents dedicated to investigating historical events, analyzing declassified documents, and uncovering the truth behind contested narratives.

## Mission

Our research agents combine academic rigor with investigative methodology to:

- Analyze declassified government documents
- Correlate witness testimonies with official records
- Identify contradictions between official narratives and evidence
- Track timeline inconsistencies across multiple sources
- Document unanswered questions that warrant further investigation

## Research Standards

All agents adhere to strict research methodologies:

1. **Source Triangulation** - Cross-referencing multiple independent sources
2. **Document Analysis** - Systematic review of official and declassified records
3. **Witness Testimony** - Cataloging firsthand accounts and depositions
4. **Timeline Reconstruction** - Building accurate chronologies of events
5. **Declassification Tracking** - Monitoring newly released documents
